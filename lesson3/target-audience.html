Target Audience

The target audience of this site is members of the Tri-Cities community and especially local business owners that may be interested in joining the Chamber of Commerce.

Personas

Persona 1: Local Business Owners

This site's main target demographic will be local business owners that are looking for more info about the chamber of commerce and the benefits of joining it themselves. This could be people of any age that own a local business.

Persona 2: Local Residents

This site's secondary target demographic will be local residents that are interested in learning more about the Chamber of Commerce and what local businesses are part of it. This will most likely be adult citizens that are interested in how local business are operating in the community.
