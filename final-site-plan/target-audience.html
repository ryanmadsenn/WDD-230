Target Audience

The target audience of this site is members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints that are have traveled for a temple wedding, or that are just traveling to visit temples. It is also for full-time missionaries that are assigned to labor in these temples.

Personas

Persona 1: Traveling Member of the Church

The main target demographic for this site will be members of the church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Whether they are traveling to attend a wedding or just to visit temples, this persona will account for the majority of this site's traffic.

Persona 2: Full-time Missionaries Assigned to Temples

The secondary target demographic for this site will be full-time missionaries that have been assigned to labor in temples. These missionaries can take advantage of the extended-stay service offered for full-time missionaries.
