Temple Spotlight

St. George Utah Temple

Dedicated in 1877, the St. George Temple was the first temple to be completed in Utah and is the oldest operating temple in the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. An interesting fact about the St. George Temple is that a cannon used by Napoleon in his siege on Moscow aided in creating the gravel for the foundation this temple stands on.
